My Professional journey

Greetings! I am a recent graduate of a full stack web development bootcamp, eager to break into the software development world. Prior to pursuing my passion for technology, I completed a Master of Commerce in Information System from the esteemed University of Queensland, where I took relevant subjects in management systems, information system analysis and design, managing SQL databases, and introduction to Python programming. After graduation, I started my career in property management and worked there for over two years. During this time, I honed my skills in communication, problem-solving, and project management, which are all transferable to my new career path.

However, my passion for technology continued to grow, and I knew I wanted to make a career change back into the information technology industry. To achieve this goal, I enrolled in a rigorous and intensive full stack web development bootcamp, where I gained expertise in front-end and back-end web development using modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. Additionally, I worked on various projects that demonstrated my ability to create responsive and user-friendly web applications. I am excited to take my newfound skills and apply them to real-world problems, building innovative solutions that can make a difference in people's lives. Thank you for considering my background, and I am eager to contribute my passion and skills to your team.
